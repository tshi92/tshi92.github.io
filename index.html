I am currently a research associate of the Tianjin Key Laboratory of Advanced Networking at Tianjin University. Before that, I received my PhD degree (in 2021) from the Massive Data Computing Lab, at Harbin Institute of Technology, advised by Prof. Jianzhong Li. I also worked as a visiting student at the George Washington University from 2019 to 2020, advised by Prof. Xiuzhen Cheng.

My research interests include sensor networks, battery-free networks and edge computing.
